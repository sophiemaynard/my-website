Sophie Maynard

The Artist

Based in Berkhamsted, I am a fine artist specialising in contemporary portraiture. My work seeks to find the balance between technical precision and the raw, emotional character of my subjects.

The Philosophy

I believe that every painting is a conversation. Whether it is a pet portrait or a landscape, my goal is to distill the essence of the subject—capturing not just a likeness, but a moment in time that feels permanent and tactile.
