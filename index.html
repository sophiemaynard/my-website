The Artist

From my studio in Berkhamsted, I create works that serve as the emotional and aesthetic anchor of a room. I specialise in pet portraiture that honors the unique bond you share with your animals. My goal is to create a meaningful tribute that feels as alive as the subject itself.

The Philosophy

I view every commission as a site-specific dialogue. A work of art must resonate with the architecture and atmosphere it inhabits. By considering light, scale, and the intended energy of a room, I ensure each piece becomes an intentional extension of the interior.
