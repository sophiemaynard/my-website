Capturing Your Moments

The Artist’s Eye

From my studio in Berkhamsted, I create works that serve as the emotional and aesthetic anchor of a room. A portrait should capture more than a physical likeness; it should reflect the character of a companion.

I also create site-specific artwork designed to suit the atmosphere of a room. Whether it is a personal residence or a professional environment, I believe art should be the focal point that brings a space together.

To ensure each project receives the focus it requires, I accept only a select number of commissions each year.

The Philosophy

More Than A Likeness

I view every commission as a site-specific dialogue. A work of art must resonate with the architecture and atmosphere it inhabits.

By considering light, scale, and the intended energy of a room, I ensure each piece becomes an intentional extension of the interior.
