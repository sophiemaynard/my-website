Sophie Maynard

The Artist

Based in Berkhamsted, my studio creates bespoke artwork tailored for both private collections and curated environments. My practice is driven by a lifelong love for animals, the outdoors, and a deep appreciation for intentional design. I specialise in high-end pet portraiture that captures the character of a companion. My work in this field is an extension of my own life in the countryside, inspired by the my whippet, Trudy, and my horse, Geenie. Beyond portraiture, I create site-specific artwork for all environments. From the sanctuary of a private home to the distinct atmosphere of a professional space, I believe art should define the identity of the room it inhabits. With a curated number of commissions accepted each year, I work closely with patrons to ensure every piece, whether a personal tribute or a centerpiece for a space, is perfectly suited to its surroundings.

The Philosophy

I believe every painting is a conversation. My goal is to distill the essence of the subject—capturing not just a likeness, but a moment in time that feels permanent and tactile.
